# Quick Start Guide - Zombie Swamp

## Getting Started in 3 Easy Steps

### 1. Open the Game
- Double-click `index.html` to open in your default browser
- OR right-click `index.html` ‚Üí "Open with" ‚Üí Choose your browser

### 2. Start Playing
- Click "Start Game" button
- You'll spawn in the center of the grid (green circle)
- Zombies (red circles) spawn around you
- Swamps (blue tiles) are scattered across the grid

### 3. Move and Survive
- **Listen** for the ticking clock sound
- When you hear the tick, you can move
- Use **Arrow Keys** or **WASD** to move one tile
- Lead zombies into swamps to eliminate them
- Don't touch zombies or step into swamps!

## Quick Tips

### Movement Timing
- The ticking clock tells you when you can move
- Both you and zombies move once per second
- You see a glow around the game board when you can move
- Plan your next move while waiting for the tick

### Strategy Basics
1. **Keep Distance**: Don't let zombies get too close
2. **Use Corners**: Lead zombies along edges toward swamps
3. **Chain Kills**: Eliminate zombies quickly for combo bonuses
4. **Plan Ahead**: Think 2-3 moves ahead

## Common Questions

**Q: Why can't I move?**
A: You can only move once per second when you hear the tick sound. Wait for the next tick!

**Q: How do I eliminate zombies?**
A: Lead them into swamps. Zombies always move toward you, so position yourself so they step into swamps.

**Q: What happens if I die?**
A: You lose one life and respawn in the center. When all lives are gone, it's game over.

**Q: How do I win a level?**
A: Eliminate all zombies by luring them into swamps.

**Q: Is there sound?**
A: Yes! Make sure your browser has sound enabled. You'll hear:
- Ticking clock for movement timing
- Splash sounds when zombies drown
- Combo sounds for consecutive kills
- Ambient zombie groans

## Troubleshooting

### No Sound?
- Check your browser's sound settings
- Some browsers block sound on first load - click anywhere on the page
- Adjust your system volume

### Game Not Loading?
- Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
- Make sure JavaScript is enabled
- Check browser console for errors (F12)

### Game Running Slow?
- Close other browser tabs
- Try a different browser
- Reduce browser window size
